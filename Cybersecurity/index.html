CyberSecurity

Computer security, cybersecurity or information technology security (IT security) is the protection of computer systems and networks from information disclosure, theft of or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data, as well as from the disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.

Data Protection

With the broad development of information technologies, the profile of this guarantee changed slowly as personal information began to represent the person in countless situations, making it necessary to develop an instrument for the effective control of this information so that it could in the end protect the person himself.

White Hat

A white hat (or a white hat hacker) is an ethical computer hacker, or a computer security expert, who specializes in penetration testing and other testing methodologies that ensure the security of an organization's information systems.
